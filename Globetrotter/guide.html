Guide to the Bahamas


Pirate Republic Brewing

Address: Woodes Rogers Walk, Nassau, New Providence

This lively craft brewery, inspired by Nassau's pirate history, offers a range of locally brewed beers with unique flavors like their popular Golden Ale and Black Beer’d Stout. Stop by for a brewery tour, sample tasting flights, and a taste of island-inspired pub fare. It’s a perfect spot to enjoy live music and mingle with locals and travelers alike.

Dean’s Blue Hole

Address: West of Clarence Town, Long Island

One of the Bahamas’ natural wonders, Dean’s Blue Hole plunges 663 feet, making it one of the deepest blue holes in the world. This site is beloved by snorkelers, divers, and adventurers who come for its pristine, turquoise waters, rich marine life, and breathtaking views. A nearby beach offers a serene spot to unwind or picnic, making it a must-visit for nature lovers.

The Island House

Address: Mahogany Hill, Western Road, Nassau

An upscale boutique hotel with a strong commitment to eco-friendly luxury, The Island House offers everything from an art-house cinema and yoga classes to tennis courts and fine dining. Visitors can experience true Bahamian hospitality with modern touches, from chic rooms to farm-to-table cuisine at its restaurants. A great choice for those looking for relaxation with a touch of adventure.
